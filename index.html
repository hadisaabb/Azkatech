Part 1
Problem understanding & root-cause thinking
A 40% surge in support tickets, abandoned CRM, and Excel workarounds are not isolated software bugs. They are lagging indicators of a breakdown across four dimensions: process, people, system, and governance.
Process

Over-engineered CRM workflows

Sales reps are forced to enter detailed technical site data (roof pitch, panel specs, shading metrics) at the very first lead stage. The friction is so high they bypass the CRM entirely — tracking deals in notebooks and phone notes — which starves leadership of pipeline visibility.

Fix: Strip non-essential fields from early CRM stages. Move technical surveys to the Quotation/Project phase only.
People

Generic, feature-based training

Teams were trained on what Odoo's buttons do — not how to execute their actual solar workflows. Accountants don't know how to process progressive milestone billing. Warehouse staff can't manage inverter backorders. This knowledge gap drives the helpdesk spike.

Fix: Replace generic training with role-specific, 30-minute "Day-in-the-Life" clinics built around solar workflows.
System

Missing financial layouts & page-load lag

The accounting team retreated to Excel because the CFO's required milestone billing reports don't exist natively. Separately, severe page-load delays in Inventory and Project modules (likely unindexed custom fields or unstored compute dependencies) give users the impression Odoo is broken.

Fix: Build the CFO's exact pivot views inside Odoo. Run server log audits and optimize database indexing.
Governance

No tiered support & unmanaged scope creep

Every forgotten password gets logged as a high-priority ticket directly to Azkatech — there's no internal first-line filter. Meanwhile, the client treats every post-go-live change request as already paid for, with no Change Control Board to link requests to financial impact.

Fix: Appoint Super-Users as Tier-1 triage. Establish a joint Steering Committee with a formal change control process.

Part 3
Commercial awareness & upsell thinking
The strategy is "Stabilize → Optimize → Transform." No paid pitches during the crisis phase. Once the client sees measurable results, expansion conversations open naturally — framed as unlocking value they've already paid for.
Day 45 · Unlock, not upsell
Field Service (FSM) module activation
Field engineers run on WhatsApp and paper. There's no real-time scheduling, no on-site material tracking, and no digital completion sign-off — leading to inventory "shrinkage" and manual double-entry by office staff.
20% reduction in admin overhead by eliminating manual data re-entry
15% fewer project delays through real-time GPS routing and scheduling
$5K–$12K/year saved by forcing digital inventory validation before job completion
"You're actually already paying for this in your existing Enterprise subscription. We'll activate and configure it in one week — zero additional software cost."
Day 75 · After adoption hits 80%+
Advanced inventory automation & barcoding
Solar components require serialized tracking (specific inverter serial numbers matched to customer warranties). Manual clipboard checks cause fulfillment delays, stock discrepancies, and expensive emergency procurement runs.
99% warehouse inventory accuracy, up from an estimated 75%
12% fewer procurement cycle losses via automated reorder rules
8–10 hours/week saved for warehouse manager through mobile barcode scanning
"Now that inventory moves are flowing smoothly in Odoo, the remaining bottleneck is manual input speed at the loading dock. A focused barcoding sprint will lock accuracy to 99% and eliminate stock-out delays."
Day 90 · At the quarterly business review
Strategic migration to Odoo v18
Odoo v15 is moving past standard vendor support. It lacks v18's performance architecture, native multi-company financial consolidation, and improved mobile UX — the exact things users complain about most.
Up to 40% improvement in system-wide page load — resolves the core "Odoo is slow" complaint
Eliminates legacy technical debt accumulated during the v15 customisation phase
Secures the technology infrastructure for the next five years of business growth
"Three months ago, Odoo was a cost centre. Today, tickets are down 45%, CRM adoption is universal, and project margins are visible in real time. The migration to v18 locks in these gains permanently."
